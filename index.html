About Smart Civic AI





Smart Civic AI is an AI-powered civic complaint platform designed as a
hackathon prototype.

It demonstrates how Artificial Intelligence, Speech Recognition,
Location Services and Image Upload can assist citizens in reporting
municipal issues more effectively.

The municipal workflow shown in this application is a demonstration of
how a future production system could integrate with official
government systems.
